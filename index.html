Process

Simple. Clear. No surprises.

From first call to launch, here's exactly how we work together.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a 45-minute call to understand your goals, audience, competitive landscape, and what success looks like. No pitching — just listening and asking the right questions.

02

Strategy & Proposal

I develop a tailored scope, timeline, and pricing — with clear deliverables and milestones. No vague quotes, no hourly billing surprises. You know exactly what you're getting.

03

Design & Build

I work in focused sprints with regular async check-ins. You see progress weekly — not in a big reveal at the end. Feedback loops are tight, revisions are structured, and nothing ships without your sign-off.

04

Launch & Handoff

We go live together. You get a full walkthrough, documented assets, and 2 weeks of post-launch support included. No handoff cliff — I make sure everything runs smoothly.
